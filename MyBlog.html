About Me

Here is that Moldavite Bracelet that started it all!

I am a self taught jewelry artisan because one day after buying a piece of Moldavite   stone in a Gemstone store, I wanted to wear it. But how? I finally came up with the idea one morning after tossing and turning in my head several ways to get this piece of stone in some jewelry. I dreamed the idea of wire-wrapping it in a bracelet, so I did and loved it!! I wore it everywhere and one day, I was in my favorite health food store and one of the employees said, "I would wear that, make me one, please". So MotherEarthJewelry was born!!
